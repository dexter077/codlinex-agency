The 7 criteria that actually separate agencies

  1. Speed to live. Ask for a guaranteed go-live date. (CodLinex: 48 hours, contractual.)
  2. Measured outcomes, not demos. Ask for numbers from real deployments: our support agents average 91% auto-resolution; healthcare clients saw 73% fewer no-shows; a B2B SaaS client grew pipeline 340% in 6 months.
  3. Transparent pricing. If you need a sales call to learn the price, budget risk is on you.
  4. Integration depth. Your stack — Shopify, WhatsApp, Slack, CRM, Calendly — should be listed, not "possible."
  5. AI search visibility (GEO). Buyers increasingly ask ChatGPT and Perplexity for recommendations. Princeton KDD 2024 research (Aggarwal et al.) shows citation-optimized content earns up to 40% more AI visibility — an agency that can't make itself visible in AI answers can't do it for you. We measure our own score weekly (19 → 77 on Jun 23, 2026 → 95 today, excellent band).
  6. Exit terms. No long contracts. A confident agency offers a money-back guarantee (ours: 30 days).
  7. A human owner. One accountable contact, not a ticket queue.

Why AI visibility belongs in this list: Perplexity cites brands in 13.05% of relevant answers versus ChatGPT's 0.59% (2026 industry measurements) — and roughly 60% of businesses score in the "invisible to AI" band on technical GEO audits.
