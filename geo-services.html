AI search visibility: how we measure it

Most agencies promise visibility; we measure it. CodLinex runs a proprietary weekly tracking system — the same class of tooling as enterprise platforms — that queries AI engines with your buyers' real questions and records:

  • Visibility % — share of relevant queries where your brand appears
  • Citation position — are you the first source or a footnote?
  • Sentiment — how AI characterizes your brand when it mentions you
  • Share of model — your slice of AI answers versus named competitors
  • Query coverage — exactly which questions you lose, mapped to content actions

Every engagement starts with a measured baseline and reports deltas — never vibes.
